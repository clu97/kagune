KAGUNE - A ghoul's predatory organ and functions as their weapon and claws. It is usually the color red similar to blood but it varies depending each individual. It is composed of RC cells, which flows just like blood, and can become as solid as teeth.

Ukaku

Spreads out like feathers and is released from the shoulder area. It specializes in high speed attacks. Ukakus can be crystallized to deliver high speed torrent of spike-like projectiles but it's short ranged. Ukakus are fast but they run out of stamina quickly.

Koukaku

Released below the shoulder blade. Due to its high density of RC cells, it is heavy and extremely robust, giving it the greatest sturdiness and making them very suited for defense. Speed is low due to the heavy weight.

Rinkaku

Has an appearance similar to scaled tentacles and is released at the back around the waist. A Rinkaku user has powerful regenerative abilities and can survive the most critical damage. This regenerative poweris due to the easily binding RC cells but because the binding force is weak the kagune is very soft and easy to break.

Bikaku
